Needtobreathe is a southern rock band fronted by Bear Rinehart and his brother, Bo. The band has produced six studio albums since 2006, beginning with "Daylight". Needtobreathe has achieved popularity on both the contemporary and Christian charts. While not explicitly a Christian band, Needtobreathe's discography contains several Christian songs, and the group has won several GMA Dove awards. The band has toured with several popular artists, like Taylor Swift and Tim McGraw, as well as headlining multiple tours. Some of the band's most popular songs include "Brother", featuring Gavin Degraw, "Washed by the Water", "Something Beautiful", "The Outsiders" and "The Heart."
